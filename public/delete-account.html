Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your NoResume account and associated personal data

⚡ Quickest way: delete from within the app

If you still have access to your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account inside the NoResume app. This is instant and fully automated. Use the form below only if you no longer have access to your account.

What happens when your account is deleted

  • Your profile, availability, and personal data will be removed
  • Active assignments will be closed with 48 hours notice to your employer
  • Pending job applications will be withdrawn
  • Financial and VAT records are retained for 6 years as required by UK law (HMRC)
  • You have a 30-day grace period during which you can cancel your request by logging back in

Processing time

Requests submitted via this form are processed manually within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted. For immediate deletion, use the in-app option above.
